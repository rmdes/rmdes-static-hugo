# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Data Collection

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no:

- Cookies
- Analytics trackers
- User accounts
- Contact forms that store data
- Third-party tracking scripts

## External Services

This site displays content from external services (GitHub, Bluesky, Mastodon, Spotify, YouTube). When you click links to these services, their respective privacy policies apply.

## Hosting

This website is self-hosted on a private Cloudron instance. No visitor data is logged or retained beyond standard server access logs required for security and maintenance purposes.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach me through the contact links on the homepage.
